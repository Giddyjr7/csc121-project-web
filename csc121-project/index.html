About Our Department

Excellence in Computer Science Education and Research

Our Mission

To provide world-class education in computer science, fostering innovation, critical thinking, and ethical responsibility in our students while advancing the frontiers of computing research.

Faculty Excellence

Our distinguished faculty brings 20+ years of combined experience in teaching and research, with expertise spanning AI, cybersecurity, software engineering, and data science.

Modern Facilities

State-of-the-art computer labs, research facilities, and collaborative spaces equipped with the latest technology to support hands-on learning and innovation.
